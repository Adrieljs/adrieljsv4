Make It Easy.

Do you know that you can add bots to your discord server?

A discord bot usually has more functionality than a normal user has. This includes but not limited to: Music, Moderation, Chat. Discord Bots can make Managing Servers much easier and Horizon is one of them! Horizon is a giveaway, chat, moderation and a music bot.

How to invite a discord bot to a discord server:

  1. Follow the bot's invite link. (Click Here to invite Horizon)

    Step 1

    Follow the bot's invite link, in this case, Horizon's invite link:
    https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=731510386184159282&permissions=8&scope=bot


  2. Add it to one of your servers.

    Step 2

    Invite it to whatever server you want.


  3. Click continue.

    Step 3

    Click continue after you have selected the preferred server and after that, a discord bot should join your server.
